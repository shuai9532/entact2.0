Register Information

If you do not have a Google account, please click the Register button and select Create Account. This will allow you to create a new Google account with ease.

Log In Information

You may enter any email that is linked with Google. For example: tppetrov@ucsd.edu or teodorppetrov@gmail.com. It must be a real, valid existing account.

What Can I Do On This Site?

Once you are logged into your Google account, you will be given options in the sidebar menu which allow you to create and browse events. You may also check the events that you have created and joined.

Why Did My Event Not Get Created?

Sometimes when it is your first time logging in with Google on our site, it does not realize that there is a signed in user and it does not write it to the database. However, just fill it out a second time and submit the event.
